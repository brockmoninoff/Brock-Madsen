a little bit about me

I'm a Utah native with a somewhat unconventional pathway into the tech field. I got my undergraduate degree in mechanical engineering, but it didn't stick. I had so many interests and talents that I felt were going unused, so I decided to make a switch. Because I've always had a knack for computers, I decided to give UX and web design a try.

The more I get into design, the more I enjoy it. It's always exciting to create something out of thin air. And I gain an immense amount of fulfillment bringing my own and others' ideas to life. I'm naturally both creative and analytical, so I'm well-equipt to solve design problems. In my free-time, you can find me playing music or enjoying the great outdoors.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS
